Parents and guardians are a major part of the Fusion FC environment.

## On matchdays
- Arrive early and be prepared.
- Encourage effort and teamwork.
- Let coaches coach and referees officiate.

## Sideline standards
We ask all supporters to keep the touchline positive and player-focused.

## Communication
If your child cannot attend training or fixtures, notify the team coach as early as possible.
