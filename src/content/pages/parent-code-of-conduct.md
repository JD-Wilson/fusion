Parents and guardians set the tone around the pitch. This code helps us create a safe, positive, and respectful environment for every child.

## Support all players
- Encourage effort, learning, and teamwork.
- Celebrate good play from both teams.
- Never criticise or embarrass a child from the touchline.

## Respect coaches and officials
- Let coaches coach and referees referee.
- Do not shout tactical instructions during matches.
- Accept decisions without abuse or confrontation.

## Matchday behaviour
- Arrive on time and prepared.
- Use positive language and stay calm.
- Keep the sideline safe, tidy, and free from conflict.

## Communication standards
- Raise concerns privately and respectfully with the coach.
- Avoid difficult conversations during or immediately after a match.
- Use club channels for updates about attendance, availability, and issues.

## Online and social conduct
- Be respectful in group chats and social media posts.
- Do not post content that criticises children, coaches, referees, or opponents.
- Check permissions before sharing photos of players.

## Working together
When adults model respect and self-control, players enjoy football more and develop faster.
