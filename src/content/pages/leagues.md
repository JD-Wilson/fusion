Fusion teams compete in local and regional leagues selected to match player development needs.

## Competition priorities
- Structured fixtures with fair progression.
- Positive environments for players to learn under pressure.
- Transparent communication around schedules and expectations.

League assignments may evolve each season based on team growth and registration updates.
