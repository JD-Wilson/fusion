Fusion FC currently supports multiple age groups and continues to expand each season.

## Development approach
- Age-appropriate sessions with clear weekly themes.
- Matchday plans focused on learning, not just results.
- Consistent communication with parents and guardians.

Players can progress internally as they grow, with support from coaches and club coordinators.
