Fusion FC is built on clear values that guide every training session, fixture, and interaction.

## Respect
We respect referees, opponents, coaches, and teammates.

## Commitment
We show up prepared, on time, and ready to improve.

## Growth mindset
We treat mistakes as learning opportunities and celebrate progress.

## Community
We support one another and represent the club with pride.
