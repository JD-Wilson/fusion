At Fusion FC, we want football to be fun, safe, and respectful for everyone. These are our team rules for U10 players.

## We show respect
- We listen when coaches or referees speak.
- We are kind to teammates, opponents, and spectators.
- We use good language and good manners.

## We work hard
- We arrive on time and ready to train.
- We try our best, even when things feel difficult.
- We keep going and learn from mistakes.

## We play fair
- We follow the rules of the game.
- We do not cheat, dive, or argue.
- We shake hands and say "well played" after matches.

## We are good teammates
- We pass, communicate, and include everyone.
- We clap effort, not just goals.
- We help each other improve.

## We stay safe and responsible
- We wear the right kit and shin pads.
- We bring water and look after our equipment.
- We tell a coach if we are hurt or worried.

## If we get it wrong
Everyone makes mistakes. We will:
1. Get a reminder.
2. Talk with the coach.
3. Involve parents if needed.

Then we reset and try again.
