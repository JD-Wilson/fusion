This quarter, every age group will focus on becoming more confident in possession and sharper in decision-making.

## Core themes
- First touch quality in tight spaces
- Passing with purpose and timing
- Recovery runs and defensive awareness

We expect these themes to improve both individual confidence and team cohesion.
