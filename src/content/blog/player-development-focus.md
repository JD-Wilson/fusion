This quarter, every age group will focus on becoming more confident in possession and sharper in decision-making. With sixteen teams training regularly across U8, U9, U10, and U11, our coaching group has aligned on shared themes while leaving room for each coach to tailor sessions to their squad's specific needs.

## Core themes
- First touch quality in tight spaces
- Passing with purpose and timing
- Recovery runs and defensive awareness

## Age group focus

**U8s** are working on the fundamentals — comfortable receiving the ball, finding a teammate, and enjoying the game. At this age, confidence and enjoyment come first.

**U9s** are building on those foundations with more structured small-sided games, introducing the idea of shape and simple combinations.

**U10s** are developing positional awareness and starting to apply decision-making under pressure. With multiple squads at this level — including our playing-up groups in the Staffordshire Junior Football League — there is a healthy range of challenge available.

**U11s** are working on the transition between phases of play — how to move from defending to attacking quickly and with purpose in the North Staffs Junior Youth League.

We expect these shared themes to improve both individual confidence and team cohesion across all squads. Coaches will communicate specific session focus areas to parents at the start of each week.
