Fusion FC is proud to announce new sponsor partners supporting our team kit plans for the 2026/27 season.

With sixteen teams now registered across the Staffordshire Junior Football League and the North Staffs Junior Youth League, keeping players properly equipped is a significant undertaking. The backing from our partners makes a real difference — from matchday shirts to training tops to wet weather gear for winter sessions.

## Sponsor impact
- Kit coverage across Saturday and Sunday squads
- Additional teamwear support for U8, U9, U10, and U11 groups
- Better wet weather gear availability for winter blocks
- Greater flexibility for replacement kit orders throughout the season

Every child who pulls on a Fusion shirt on a Saturday or Sunday morning does so because local businesses are willing to invest in the community. We are genuinely grateful for that support.

Thank you to every local business backing grassroots football in Staffordshire.
