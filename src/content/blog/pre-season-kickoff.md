The new season starts now.

We have confirmed our pre-season structure across all sixteen teams, with dedicated sessions for each age group and clear focus areas for technical growth, shape, and decision-making.

This season we are competing on both days of the weekend. Our six Saturday sides compete in the Staffordshire Junior Football League, while our ten Sunday sides are registered in the North Staffs Junior Youth League. Coaches across every team have been working on their pre-season plans and players can expect a structured, purposeful start to the campaign.

## This month
- Fitness and movement foundations
- Passing and receiving under pressure
- Defensive transitions and team shape

## Saturday squads
Our Saturday programme runs across U8, U9, and U10 age groups, with two playing-up squads giving our more advanced players the challenge of competing at a higher level.

## Sunday squads
The Sunday programme covers U8 through to U11, with multiple teams at U9 and U10 reflecting the strength and depth across those year groups.

Parents will receive schedule reminders from team coaches each week. If you have any questions about which squad your child is part of, please speak directly to your team coach or get in touch through the contact page.
